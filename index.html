What is ProPredict?

Updated March 9, 2024

ProPredict strives to inspire people of all ages to take action. Whether it be testing for yourself, or encouraging others, we believe knowledge is power. Our products aim to help predict early medical issues so individuals can seek the right prevention and treatment methods. ProPredict is currently focusing on AlzPredict, a noninvasive, accurate, and efficient solution to screen for early Alzheimer's. Our team consists of students across Cornell, University of Connecticut, and Northeastern. We specialize in Computer Science, Nutrition, Finance, BioMedical Engineering, Marketing, and Information Science. ProPredict uses Machine Learning along with Mass Spectrometry and Proteomics to produce a test for early detection.
